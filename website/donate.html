Help support the Indie Python project

There are many ways to support the Indie Python project and its apps with donations:

Method Type Description
Patreon Recurring crowdfunding The primary way to help the project is to support us on patreon. This also gives you access to exclusive discord channels and voting rights on specific pools related to our apps/game projects.
Liberapay Recurring crowdfunding Same benefits as patrons from Patreon, just make sure to reach me on discord so that I can assign your role according to your donation.
Ko-fi One time donations + recurring crowdfunding For one time donations, you can use the Ko-fi account. Ko-fi also offers recurring crowdfunding. Since this platform is great for one time donations, expect to see links to this account spread throughout Indie Python content in strategic non-disrupting spots. For recurring crowdfunding, users have the same benefits as patrons from Patreon, just make sure to reach me on discord so that I can assign your role according to your donation.
Pix One time donations Only available in Brazil

If there is another method of your preference, please, contact us by email (kennedy@kennedyrichard.com) so that we can discuss it and maybe even make the method available here.
